Why We Boycott Trump’s Backers

Trump’s 2024 campaign threatens our future. Here’s why we resist:

  • Undermining Democracy: His policies and rhetoric promote division and weaken democratic institutions, backed by billionaires who profit from deregulation.
  • Climate Denial: Supported by fossil fuel tycoons like Harold Hamm, Trump’s agenda stalls renewable energy and exacerbates the climate crisis.
  • Economic Inequality: Tax breaks for the ultra-wealthy, funded by donors like Miriam Adelson, deepen the wealth gap while working families struggle.
  • Social Injustice: His campaign amplifies hate, threatening marginalized communities, with corporate donors enabling this agenda.

By boycotting their companies, we hit them where it hurts—their profits. Join us to send a message: our future isn’t for sale.
